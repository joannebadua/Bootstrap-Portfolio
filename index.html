About Me

>My name is Joey Badua. I go by the pronouns she/they.

I was born in the Philippines, and grew up in San Francisco, California and Honolulu, Hawaii.

When I'm not coding, I practice yoga.

Before I was a web developer, I practiced law as a deputy public defender in Hawaii, working in the appellate and trial divisions. I have tried felony cases in juvenile court. I represented clients facing involuntary commitments in mental hospitals, as well as convicted inmates in minimum, parole and parole revocation hearings. I worked with clients from all walks of life, and was able to put myself in their shoes to represent them.

My goal is to build programs that make all people accessible to content relevant to them.
